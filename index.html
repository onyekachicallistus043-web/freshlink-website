About Us

Our Story

Freshlink Hub was born out of a simple frustration — the everyday hassle of buying groceries in Nigeria. Long hours at crowded markets, unpredictable prices, and questionable quality shouldn't be the price of feeding your family well.

We built Freshlink Hub to change that. Our digital grocery marketplace brings quality, verified grocery products directly to your screen — and then to your doorstep — with speed, transparency, and care.

From Lagos to the rest of Nigeria, we're on a mission to make fresh, affordable groceries accessible to every household. No stress. No market hassle. Just fresh food, delivered.
